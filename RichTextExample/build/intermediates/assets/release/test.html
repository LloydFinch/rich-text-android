Cursing, references to Guinness and barely legible accents. The three pillars of the stereotypical Irishman in the American consciousness. Now we could get on our uppity high horse about that or we could just watch these two Irish chaps on their J1 holiday in Texas and just enjoy it for the fun it is. The moment Cillian realises he's cursed on television and his mammy might see it is just wonderful. For the sake of Cillian and Shane's pockets, it's probably a good thing that the Rangers ended up losing 12-6 to the Angels. And before anyone says anything, the 'good day mate' is apparently a bit that the interviewer, Jim Knox, does whenever he interviews someone who's not American.
